This Privacy Policy explains how the QR Scanner app ("the App") handles information. We built this app to work entirely on your device, with no account, no cloud sync, and no data sent to us.

Camera access

The App requests camera permission solely to scan QR codes and barcodes in real time. Camera frames are processed on-device to detect and decode codes. No camera image or video is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere — frames are discarded immediately after each scan attempt.

Scan history

When you scan a code or generate a QR code, the decoded or encoded content (text, URL, or barcode value) and the timestamp are saved in a local database stored only on your device. This history is never uploaded to any server. Deleting an entry or clearing history permanently removes it from your device with no remote copy retained, since none exists.

Generated QR code images

QR codes you generate and save are written as image files to your device's local app storage. These files stay on your device unless you explicitly share them using your device's share sheet, at which point the destination is controlled entirely by you and the app you choose to share with.

Permissions requested

  • Camera — required for scanning. The App will not function without it.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. It does not communicate with any external server as part of its core scanning, history, or generation features.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children, since no information is collected or transmitted in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes (for example, if a future version adds optional cloud sync or AI features), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change. Material changes affecting how data is handled will be clearly described here before being shipped to users.
