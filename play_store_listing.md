## Short description (80 char max)

## Full description (4000 char max)

Scan any QR code or barcode instantly with a fast, clean, ad-free scanner.

**Scan everything**
Point your camera at any QR code or common barcode format — EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A, UPC-E, Code 128, Code 39, and more. Detection is instant, with a clear scan frame and flashlight toggle for low-light scanning.

**Clear results, every time**
See exactly what was scanned, when, and what type of code it was. Copy the result, share it instantly, or open links directly — no extra taps, no clutter.

**Your scan history, organized**
Every scan is automatically saved locally on your device. Search past scans, delete individual entries, or clear everything in one tap. Nothing is ever uploaded — your history stays on your phone.

**Generate your own QR codes**
Turn any text or link into a QR code in seconds. Preview it live as you type, then save it as an image or share it directly.

**Built for speed and privacy**
- No account required
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics
- Works fully offline
- Camera frames are never stored or transmitted — everything is processed on your device
- Clean dark mode support

Whether you're scanning a restaurant menu, a product barcode, a Wi-Fi QR code, or generating one to share, this app gets it done quickly with a modern, distraction-free interface.
